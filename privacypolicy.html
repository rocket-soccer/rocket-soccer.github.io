Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/22/2024

Introduction

At Rocket Soccer, accessible from rocket-soccer.github.io, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information we collect and record, and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Game progress and preferences
  • Device information and browser type
  • Gameplay statistics and scores
  • User-provided information (when applicable)

How We Use Your Data

  • To improve game performance and user experience
  • To save your game progress and preferences
  • To maintain leaderboards and achievements
  • To provide technical support when needed

Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored securely and accessed only when necessary.

Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your gaming experience. These cookies store your preferences and game progress. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect some game features.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will promptly remove such information.
